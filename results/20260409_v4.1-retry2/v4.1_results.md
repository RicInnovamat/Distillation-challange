## Harness Fixes Applied

1. **temperature=1.0 for reasoning models** — gpt-oss-20b/120b now use per-model `params.temperature: 1.0` in `config/models.yaml`. Root cause: OpenAI reasoning models silently fail with temperature=0 on OpenRouter.

2. **max_tokens=65536 for reasoning models** — gpt-oss-20b averaged 9,215 output tokens; 48/346 responses hit the old 32K ceiling. Reasoning tokens count against the limit.

3. **Per-request hard timeout (180s)** — Replaced buggy `asyncio.wait_for` (which included semaphore queue wait) with per-request `aiohttp.ClientTimeout(total=180)`.

4. **Null response body guard** — Handles `resp.json()` returning `None` from OpenRouter.

5. **Empty response retry** — Detects 0-token responses and retries instead of silently accepting them.

6. **Longer retry backoff** — Changed from [2, 5, 15]s to [5, 15, 30]s.
